Welcome to Life with Lee, my cozy corner of the internet where I share my mental health journey with honesty and heart. I know how overwhelming and isolating it can feel to struggle with your mind - I've been there too. But I want you to know that you're not alone, and that there is hope for healing and happiness. Here, I'll share my stories, my struggles, and my successes, in the hopes that it might bring you comfort, reassurance, and inspiration to keep moving forward. Grab a cup of tea, get comfy, and let's navigate this wild ride called life together.Remember,You are seen, you are heard, and you are loved

On Life with Lee, we'll explore the intricacies of mental health, confront stigmas, and foster a community that uplifts and supports one another. Through honest conversations, relatable stories, and expert insights, we'll work together to :Break down barriers surrounding mental health discussions, empower individuals to take control of their well-being, share practical tools and strategies for mental wellness, celebrate resilience and the human spirit.

Join me on this journey, and let's redefine what it means to live a life that's authentic, compassionate, and wholeheartedly yours.
